# HR Handbook

## Vacation Policy
- Full-time employees receive **20 paid vacation days per year**.
- Unused vacation days cannot be carried over to the next year.
- Requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance in the HR portal.

## Public Holidays
The company observes 10 public holidays each year:
- New Year’s Day
- Independence Day
- Thanksgiving (Thu & Fri)
- Christmas (Dec 25)
- … (see HR portal for full list).

## Parental Leave
- Primary caregivers: up to **16 weeks paid leave**.
- Secondary caregivers: up to **8 weeks paid leave**.
- Leave requests should be submitted at least 1 month in advance.
- Flexible return-to-work arrangements may be discussed with HR.
