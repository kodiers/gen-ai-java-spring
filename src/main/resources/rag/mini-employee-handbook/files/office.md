# Office Handbook

## Opening Hours
- Monday–Friday: **08:00–20:00**
- Weekends & holidays: Closed  
  Access outside normal hours requires manager approval.

## Badge Policy
- Always wear your badge visibly in the office.
- Lost badges must be reported immediately.
- Visitors receive temporary badges at reception.
- Sharing badges is strictly prohibited.

## Visitor Rules
- Visitors must be pre-registered in the Visitor System.
- Visiting hours: **09:00–18:00**.
- After 18:00, visitors are not allowed without VP approval.
- Hosts are responsible for escorting visitors at all times.

## Pets
- Pets are **not allowed** in the office.
- Exceptions only for registered service animals.
