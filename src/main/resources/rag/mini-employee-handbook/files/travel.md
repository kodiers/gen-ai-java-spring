# Travel Handbook

## Booking Tool
- All business travel must be booked through the **Company Travel Portal**.
- Preferred airlines and hotels are automatically suggested.
- Bookings outside the system are not reimbursed.

## Per-Diem Limits
- Domestic travel: **$60/day**
- International travel: **$100/day**  
  This covers meals, transport, and incidentals.  
  Receipts are required for amounts above $25.

## Approval Flow
1. Employee submits travel request in the portal.
2. Manager reviews and approves.
3. Finance team confirms budget.
4. Booking is finalized.

Last-minute travel (<24h notice) requires director-level approval.
